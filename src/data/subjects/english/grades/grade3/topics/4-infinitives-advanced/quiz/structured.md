# 構造問題：不定詞（応用）

## 大問1：空欄補充（適語選択）（6問）

次の英文の（　）に入る最も適切な語を下から選び、記号で答えなさい。

(1) It is important ( A ) us ( B ) protect wild animals.
　a. for / to　　b. to / for　　c. of / to　　d. for / for
**解答**: a（It is important for us to protect wild animals.）

(2) I want you ( A ) help me with my research.
　a. help　　b. helped　　c. to help　　d. helping
**解答**: c（want + 人 + to + 動詞のもとの形）

(3) My parents didn't let me ( A ) out alone at night.
　a. to go　　b. go　　c. going　　d. went
**解答**: b（let + 人 + 動詞のもとの形。to なし）

(4) The teacher told us ( A ) be quiet during the test.
　a. be　　b. being　　c. to be　　d. been
**解答**: c（tell + 人 + to + 動詞のもとの形）

(5) Can you help me ( A ) this problem?
　a. to solving　　b. solved　　c. solving　　d. solve
**解答**: d（help + 人 + 動詞のもとの形。to は省略可）

(6) It is fun ( A ) me ( A ) learn about different ecosystems.
　a. for / to　　b. of / to　　c. to / for　　d. for / for
**解答**: a（It is fun for me to learn about different ecosystems.）

## 大問2：書きかえ問題（6問）

次の英文を、意味が変わらないように書きかえなさい。

(1) "Please open the window," she said to him.
　→ She ( told / asked ) him ( ) open the window.
**解答**: She told him to open the window. / She asked him to open the window.

(2) My parents said to me, "You can go to the concert."
　→ My parents ( ) me ( ) to the concert.
**解答**: My parents let me go to the concert.

(3) "Help me carry this bag," he said to her.
　→ He asked ( ) ( ) carry his bag.
**解答**: He asked her to carry his bag.

(4) Studying every day is necessary for students.
　→ It is necessary ( ) students ( ) study every day.
**解答**: It is necessary for students to study every day.

(5) "Don't be late," the coach said to us.
　→ The coach told us ( ) ( ) late.
**解答**: The coach told us not to be late.

(6) She said to me, "Please help me practice my speech."
　→ She asked me ( ) help her ( ) her speech.
**解答**: She asked me to help her practice her speech.

## 大問3：語順整序（6問）

次の語を並べかえて、正しい英文を完成させなさい。

(1) 「私たちにとって絶滅危惧種について学ぶことは大切です。」
〔 us / endangered / to / it / about / important / for / learn / animals / is 〕
**解答**: It is important for us to learn about endangered animals.

(2) 「彼女は私に彼女の発表を手伝うように頼みました。」
〔 me / asked / presentation / to / help / she / her / with 〕
**解答**: She asked me to help with her presentation.

(3) 「両親は私を遅くまで起きていることを許してくれました。」
〔 let / up / me / late / parents / stay / my 〕
**解答**: My parents let me stay up late.

(4) 「先生は私たちに教科書を読むように言いました。」
〔 to / the / told / read / teacher / textbook / us / the 〕
**解答**: The teacher told us to read the textbook.

(5) 「人々はラッコが油の流出から回復するのを手伝いました。」
〔 sea / helped / the / recover / people / from / otters / spill / oil / the 〕
**解答**: People helped sea otters recover from the oil spill.

(6) 「彼女にとって外国語を話すことは難しくありません。」
〔 her / a / is / not / for / speak / difficult / to / it / foreign / language 〕
**解答**: It is not difficult for her to speak a foreign language.

## 大問4：英作文（5問）

次の日本語を英語に直しなさい。

(1) 「私にとってチーターが絶滅の危機にあると知ることは衝撃的でした。」
**解答**: It was shocking for me to know that cheetahs are in danger of extinction.

(2) 「私は市民のみんなに自然を守るために行動してほしいです。」
**解答**: I want all citizens to take action to protect nature.

(3) 「彼女は私に彼女のスピーチのために情報を集めるのを手伝うように頼みました。」
**解答**: She asked me to help her collect information for her speech.

(4) 「私のお兄さんは私が自転車の修理をするのを手伝ってくれました。」
**解答**: My brother helped me fix my bicycle.

(5) 「先生は私たちにこの問題について記事を書くように言いました。」
**解答**: The teacher told us to write an article about this problem.

## 大問5：対話文・長文読解（5問）

次の対話文を読んで、あとの問いに答えなさい。

---

Ms. Green: Our topic today is endangered animals. It is very important for us to understand why so many species are disappearing.

Kenji: I read an article about cheetahs. People told me that they are critically endangered.

Ms. Green: That's right. Habitat loss and overhunting are the main reasons. The government wants everyone to know about this challenge.

Yuki: What can we do? I want to help animals survive.

Ms. Green: Good question! You can join a campaign or share information. Let me tell you about one successful case. People helped sea otters live safely after an oil spill by cleaning them and protecting their habitat.

Kenji: That's amazing. Can I help with a local wildlife campaign?

Ms. Green: Of course! I'll ask the members to welcome you.

---

(1) Ms. Greenによると、なぜ多くの種が絶滅しているのか。2つ答えなさい。
**解答**: 生息地の喪失（Habitat loss）と過剰な狩猟（overhunting）

(2) 下線部「The government wants everyone to know about this challenge.」の日本語訳を答えなさい。
**解答**: 政府はすべての人にこの課題を知ってほしいと思っています。

(3) People helped sea otters live safely. における、help のあとの動詞の形は何か？
**解答**: 動詞のもとの形（原形）。to なし。

(4) "I'll ask the members to welcome you." を日本語に訳しなさい。
**解答**: メンバーにあなたを歓迎するように頼みます。

(5) この対話から読み取れる、絶滅危惧種を守るための行動を日本語で2つ挙げなさい。
**解答**: キャンペーンに参加する、情報を広める（動物を直接助ける行動なども可）
