# 発展問題：現在完了進行形（have been + ~ing）

## 大問1: 対話文読解（4点×5＝20点）

次の対話文を読んで、あとの問いに答えなさい。

Ken: You look tired, Yuki. What have you been doing?
Yuki: I ( ① ) been practicing basketball since 3 p.m. We have a big game next week.
Ken: That's a long time! How long has your coach been ( ② ) the team?
Yuki: He has been teaching us for two years. He ( ③ ) our school since 2024.
Ken: I've been ( ④ ) about joining the team. Can I come to practice tomorrow?
Yuki: Of course! We've been waiting for new members.

(1) ①に入る最も適切な語を選びなさい。
ア am  イ was  ウ have  エ has
**答え: ウ have（主語は I なので have been practicing）**

(2) ②に入る最も適切な語を選びなさい。
ア teach  イ teaching  ウ taught  エ teaches
**答え: イ teaching（has been teaching で現在完了進行形）**

(3) ③に入る最も適切な語句を選びなさい。
ア has been knowing  イ has known  ウ is knowing  エ have known
**答え: イ has known（know は状態動詞なので現在完了。He だから has known）**

(4) ④に入る最も適切な語を選びなさい。
ア think  イ thought  ウ thinking  エ thinks
**答え: ウ thinking（have been thinking で「ずっと考えている」）**

(5) 本文の内容に合うものを選びなさい。
ア Yuki has been practicing since this morning.
イ The coach has been teaching for two years.
ウ Ken has already joined the basketball team.
エ Yuki doesn't want Ken to come to practice.
**答え: イ（He has been teaching us for two years. から）**

## 大問2: 誤文訂正（4点×5＝20点）

次の英文にはそれぞれ1か所まちがいがあります。まちがいを指摘し、正しく書きかえなさい。

(1) She have been studying English for three hours.
**答え: have → has（主語が She なので has を使う）**

(2) I has been waiting for you since 2 p.m.
**答え: has → have（主語が I なので have を使う）**

(3) He has been know her for five years.
**答え: been know → known（know は状態動詞なので現在完了 has known にする。現在完了進行形にはしない）**

(4) They have been swiming in the pool for an hour.
**答え: swiming → swimming（swim の~ing形は m を重ねて swimming）**

(5) How long have you been wait for the bus?
**答え: wait → waiting（have been + ~ing の形にする。wait → waiting）**

## 大問3: 時制の選択（4点×4＝16点）

次の（ ）から最も適切なものを選びなさい。

(1) My mother has (been cooking, cooked, cooking) since I came home.
**答え: been cooking（「帰ってからずっと料理し続けている」動作の継続 → 現在完了進行形）**

(2) He (is playing, played, has been playing) the game in his room since this morning.
**答え: has been playing（since this morning があるので現在完了進行形）**

(3) We (wait, waited, have been waiting) for you for ten minutes.
**答え: have been waiting（for ten minutes があるので現在完了進行形）**

(4) Lucy has (been, be, being) interested in anime since she first watched one.
**答え: been（interested は状態 → 現在完了 has been interested）**

## 大問4: 書きかえ問題（4点×4＝16点）

次の文を（ ）内の指示にしたがって書きかえなさい。

(1) I have been reading this book for three days.（疑問文に）
**答え: Have you been reading this book for three days?**

(2) She has been dancing since this morning.（否定文に）
**答え: She has not (hasn't) been dancing since this morning.**

(3) They have been waiting for two hours.（下線部をたずねる疑問文に。下線部＝for two hours）
**答え: How long have they been waiting?**

(4) He started running at 3 p.m. He is still running now.（現在完了進行形を使って1文に）
**答え: He has been running since 3 p.m.**

## 大問5: 総合英作文（5点×5＝25点）

次の場面にあう英文を、指定された条件を使って書きなさい。

(1) 友達が疲れた顔をしている。「どのくらいずっと勉強しているの？」と聞きたい。（How longを使って）
**答え: How long have you been studying?**

(2) 「私は今朝からずっとかばんを探していますが、見つかりません。」（現在完了進行形 + but を使って）
**答え: I have been looking for my bag since this morning, but I can't find it.**

(3) 「エリはもう宿題を終えました。」（現在完了〈完了〉を使って。※進行形ではない）
**答え: Eri has already finished her homework.**

(4) 「彼女は今朝からずっとダンスの練習をしています。」（現在完了進行形を使って）
**答え: She has been practicing dancing since this morning.**

(5) 「あなたは音楽が好きですか。」「はい。私は20年間ずっとバイオリンを弾いています。」（対話文で書け）
**答え: Do you like music? — Yes. I've been playing the violin for twenty years.**
