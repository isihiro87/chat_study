# 大問小問形式：関係代名詞（who / which / that）

## 大問1: 空欄補充（3点×6＝18点）

次の（ ）に入る最も適切な関係代名詞を who, which, that から選んで書きなさい。

(1) The man ( ) lives next door is a pilot.
**答え: who（または that）（人を説明する主格の関係代名詞）**

(2) I want a computer ( ) is fast and light.
**答え: which（または that）（ものを説明する主格の関係代名詞）**

(3) She has a cat ( ) has blue eyes.
**答え: which（または that）（動物を説明する主格の関係代名詞）**

(4) The boy ( ) won the race is my classmate.
**答え: who（または that）（人を説明する主格の関係代名詞）**

(5) This is the park ( ) we visited last summer.
**答え: which（または that）（ものを説明する目的格の関係代名詞）**

(6) I know a woman ( ) speaks five languages.
**答え: who（または that）（人を説明する主格の関係代名詞）**

## 大問2: 2文を関係代名詞を使って1文にする（4点×5＝20点）

次の2文を、関係代名詞（who / which / that）を使って1文に書きかえなさい。

(1) ① I have an uncle. ② He is a famous doctor.
**答え: I have an uncle who is a famous doctor.**

(2) ① This is the temple. ② It was built 500 years ago.
**答え: This is the temple which was built 500 years ago.**

(3) ① The girl is Yuki. ② She is reading a book under the tree.
**答え: The girl who is reading a book under the tree is Yuki.**

(4) ① I ate the cookies. ② My sister made them.
**答え: I ate the cookies that my sister made.**

(5) ① He has a bike. ② It can go very fast.
**答え: He has a bike which can go very fast.**

## 大問3: 語順整序（4点×6＝24点）

次の日本文にあう英文になるように、（ ）内の語（句）を並べかえなさい。

(1) ピアノを弾いている女の子はメアリーです。
( the piano / the girl / is / who / plays ) Mary.
**答え: The girl who plays the piano is Mary.**

(2) 私はたくさんの写真がある本を持っています。
I ( a book / many pictures / have / has / that ).
**答え: I have a book that has many pictures.**

(3) あなたが昨日買ったかばんはどこですか。
Where ( the bag / is / you / that / bought ) yesterday?
**答え: Where is the bag that you bought yesterday?**

(4) 英語を教えてくれる先生が好きです。
I ( who / the teacher / teaches / like / me ) English.
**答え: I like the teacher who teaches me English.**

(5) これは日本で作られた車です。
This ( which / a car / is / was / in Japan / made ).
**答え: This is a car which was made in Japan.**

(6) 動物園に行くバスはもう出発しました。
( that / to / the bus / goes / the zoo ) has already left.
**答え: The bus that goes to the zoo has already left.**

## 大問4: 和文英訳（5点×4＝20点）

次の日本文を、関係代名詞を使って英語にしなさい。

(1) 私にはオーストラリアに住んでいるおばがいます。
**答え: I have an aunt who lives in Australia.**

(2) これは母が作ったケーキです。
**答え: This is the cake that my mother made.**

(3) 彼女は多くの人に愛されている歌手です。
**答え: She is a singer who is loved by many people.**

(4) 私が昨日見た映画はとてもおもしろかったです。
**答え: The movie that I watched yesterday was very interesting.**

## 大問5: 英文の書きかえ（4点×5＝20点）

次の英文を、指示にしたがって書きかえなさい。

(1) This is the book that I read last week.（that を which に書きかえ）
**答え: This is the book which I read last week.**

(2) I have a friend that plays basketball well.（that を who に書きかえ）
**答え: I have a friend who plays basketball well.**

(3) The man I saw at the station was Mr. Tanaka.（省略されている関係代名詞を補って書きかえ）
**答え: The man that I saw at the station was Mr. Tanaka.（who/that/whom のいずれも可）**

(4) The picture you painted is beautiful.（関係代名詞 that を補って書きかえ）
**答え: The picture that you painted is beautiful.**

(5) I know a student who can run very fast.（that を使って同じ意味に書きかえ）
**答え: I know a student that can run very fast.**
